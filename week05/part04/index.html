Normals & smoothness (Part 4 explanation)

How we obtain normals: the OBJ provides vertex normals (vn). We upload them as a vertex attribute to the GPU.

How we use normals: normals are interpolated across the triangle, so we re-normalize per fragment and use them in diffuse/specular lighting.

Smoothness: interpolated vertex normals give smooth-looking shading. Using one face normal per triangle gives a faceted (flat) look.
