Filtering choice (minification)

Earth has high-frequency details (mountains). During rotation the texture gets minified in many screen regions which causes shimmering/aliasing. A good compromise is: minFilter = linear + mipmapping enabled + mipmapFilter = linear (trilinear filtering). This strongly reduces shimmering without overly blurring the texture.

UV from normals

We treat the (interpolated) normal as a point on the unit sphere and compute spherical coordinates. We use atan2 for the longitude (correct signs) and acos for the latitude, then map them to (u,v) in [0,1].
